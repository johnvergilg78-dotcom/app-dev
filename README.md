### **Why I Love It**

1.  **Iconic Design:** The updated, complex transformation sequences were revolutionary.
2.  **Voice Acting:** Peter Cullen's voice as Optimus Prime is simply **unbeatable**.
3.  **Memorable Soundtrack:** The score perfectly captured the scale and emotion.
